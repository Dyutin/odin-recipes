Idli

Description

Idli is a traditional South Indian steamed rice cake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. It is a popular and nutritious breakfast item in India, known for its soft and spongy texture.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Â½ teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately. Soak them in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind urad dal and fenugreek seeds into a smooth batter. Grind idli rice into a slightly coarse batter. Mix both batters together.
  3. Add salt and let the batter ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours.
  4. Grease idli molds with oil or ghee. Add a pinch of baking soda to the batter and mix well.
  5. Pour the batter into the idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes until the idlis are cooked and firm.
  6. Allow the idlis to cool for a few minutes before removing them from the molds.
  7. Serve hot idlis with coconut chutney and sambar.
