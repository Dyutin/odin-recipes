Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a classic Italian-American dish featuring fettuccine pasta coated in a creamy Alfredo sauce with tender pieces of chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Â½ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until the sauce is smooth.
  3. Add cooked chicken to the sauce and heat through.
  4. Pour Alfredo sauce over the cooked fettuccine and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
