Vegetable Stir-Fry

Description

Vegetable Stir-Fry is a quick and healthy dish made by saut√©ing a variety of colorful vegetables in a flavorful sauce. It's a versatile and delicious way to enjoy vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers (assorted colors)
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water (for sauce thickening)

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir briefly, then add broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas, and mushrooms.
  3. Stir-fry vegetables for 5-7 minutes until they are crisp-tender.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce and cornstarch-water mixture. Pour over the vegetables and stir to coat.
  5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve the vegetable stir-fry over rice or noodles.
